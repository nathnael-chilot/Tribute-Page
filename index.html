Dr. Aklilu Lemma

the man who discover and campaign relentlessly for an affordable preventative against bilharzia

Here is a time line for Dr. Aklilu's life:

  • 1935 - born inJijiga, Hararghe Province, Ethiopian Empire (now Somali Region, Ethiopia)
  • 1964 - he obtained his D.Sc.
  • 1976 - He founded the Institute of Pathobiology, now known as the Aklilu Lemma Institute of Pathobiology,[4] and taught there
  • 1989 - he got Right Livelihood Award
  • 1997 - He died in the United States

"We have learned the hard way that the root problems of scientific research in Africa are not only the lack of facilities and funds but also the biases of individuals and organisations in industrialised countries."

-- Dr. Aklilu Lemma
